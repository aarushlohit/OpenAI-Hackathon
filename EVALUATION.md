# Evaluation

Hermes-X evaluation measures cyber intelligence behavior over curated scenarios.

Components:

- `ScenarioSuite`: benchmark scenarios.
- `BenchmarkRunner`: runs scenario batches.
- `EvaluationMetrics`: false positives, false negatives, correlation precision, replay consistency, workflow latency, and escalation accuracy.
- `ReplayBenchmark`: replay verification benchmark wrapper.

Initial scenarios:

- Telegram refund scams.
- Fake offer letters.
- Cloned domains.
- Recruiter impersonation.
