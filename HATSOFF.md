# HATSOFF Protocol

HATSOFF is the Hermes-X principle for humane defensive security:

- Help students verify opportunities without shaming victims.
- Attribute risk to evidence, not assumptions.
- Treat every artifact as hostile until proven otherwise.
- Preserve original evidence without modifying it.
- Separate confidence, severity, and recommended action.
- Make every automated conclusion reviewable by a human.

The system should be fast and decisive, but never careless with reputational or personal consequences.
